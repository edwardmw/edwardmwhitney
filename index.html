Hi, I'm Edward Whitney and I study the economics of development.

I spend most of my time as a PhD student in the Agricultural Resource Economics program at UC Davis. Before returning to school, I worked for three years at International Food Policy Research Institute as a research analyst, focusing on poverty analysis in Pakistan. I got to help out with the design and training of household surveys, as well as the gritty process of getting the data ready for analysis.

When I'm not occupied with coursework, I like to explore new-to-me corners of the world and think about what societies will look like in the future.

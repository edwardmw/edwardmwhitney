Hi, I'm Edward Whitney and I study the economics of development.

I spend most of my time as a PhD student in the Agricultural and Resource Economics program at UC Davis. Before returning to school, I worked for three years at the International Food Policy Research Institute as a research analyst, focusing mostly on poverty analysis in Pakistan. I have a master's degree in International Development from American University and a bachelor's degree in Economics from the University of Utah.

When I'm not occupied with coursework, I like to explore new-to-me corners of the world and think about urban/rural linkages and global futurities.
